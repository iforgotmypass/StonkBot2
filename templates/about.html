about

it's a stock scanner based on u/alexsmith2332's configuration from this post, containing some more improvements just to be safer

it's checking indicators such as

  • 20, 50, 200 day SMA less than current price
  • daily price change +2%
  • relative volume >3
  • current volume >1M
  • good recommendations
  • price to book ratio > 1
